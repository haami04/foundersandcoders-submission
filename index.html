Why Web Development?

  • My interest in web development is driven by several factors:

  • Firstly, it is an accessible field where one can see tangible progress with consistent practice. The immediate feedback loop in coding allows for quick learning and skill refinement, making it an ideal discipline for those who thrive on hands-on experience.

  • Secondly, web development offers diverse career opportunities, ranging from front-end design to back-end development, and even specialized roles such as UX/UI design or DevOps. This variety ensures that there is always a new challenge to tackle and a new skill to acquire.

  • Lastly, web development is inherently creative; it combines technical problem-solving with artistic design, allowing developers to create engaging and functional digital experiences! (Although, my creativity can only go so far with my limited range of front end skills,,,ಥ‿ಥ)
